Lasagna

The most delicious lasagna you'll ever have!

Ready in as little as 30 minutes, you'll have the whole family wanting seconds! The only thing cheesier than this recipe is my description of it!

Ingredients

    1 (16oz) package of lasagna noodles 1 pound lean ground beef salt and pepper to taste 1 (16oz) jar of spaghetti sauce 1 clove garlic, minced 1/2 pound shredded mozzarella cheese 1/2 pound shredded cheddar cheese 1 pint ricotta cheese

Directions

    Step 1:

    Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.

    Step 2:

    Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper; drain. Stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer 5 minutes.

    Step 3:

    In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, Cheddar and ricotta; stir well. In 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled.

    Step 4:

    Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
